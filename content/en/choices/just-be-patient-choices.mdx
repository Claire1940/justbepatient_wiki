<Section icon={Map} title="Just Be Patient Choices: Branching Paths Overview">

In the dark psychological horror visual novel *Just Be Patient*, your choices determine your dynamic with Calvin, your mysterious captor. Every decision changes how Calvin behaves towards you, altering the story's progression and final outcomes. To successfully navigate this unsettling environment, you must understand how different dialogue styles align with specific routes.

| Attitude Route | Key Behavioral Focus | Primary Story Direction |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Cooperative** | Polite, attached, submissive | Leads to A Loving Heart or Compliance endings |
| **Investigative** | Questioning, curious, observant | Unlocks A Curious Mind and medication secrets |
| **Resistant** | Angry, confrontational, escape-focused | Triggers high-tension conflicts and hostile endings |
| **Passive** | Avoidant, quiet, submissive | Results in Passivity or Mercy outcomes |
| **Survival** | Panic, flight, physical struggle | Leads to the raw, high-panic Animal ending |

<Callout type="tip" title="Pro Tip: Save Frequently">
Because the game is designed for replayability, make manual saves at the start of each chapter and right before major dialogue prompts. This allows you to quickly explore different attitude routes without restarting the entire game.
</Callout>

### Step-by-Step Ending Walkthroughs

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Unlocking A Loving Heart">
    To achieve this ending, you must show emotional attachment and cooperation. Politely greet Calvin, ask why he is staring, focus on mental health, ask why he chose you, lean into being tired, and finally, tell him you love him.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Unlocking Compliance">
    Follow a highly cooperative but emotionally distant path. Politely greet him, ask why he is staring, select mental health, ask why he chose you, choose tired, and then tell him you do not love him.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Unlocking A Curious Mind">
    Take an investigative approach. Confront him, ask why he brought you there, focus on the new medication, test the restraints, check the computer, and ask how long he has felt this way about you.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Unlocking Resistance">
    Push back against Calvin at every turn. Confront him, insult him, focus on the situation, test the restraints, ask if he plans to let you go, and choose the angry response.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Unlocking Mercy & Passivity">
    For **Mercy**, beg to be let go, focus on mental health, refuse to eat, and choose the scared response. For **Passivity**, ignore him, ask why he brought you there, choose no concerns, and ask to stay upstairs with him.
  </Step>
  <Step num="6" title="Unlocking Animal">
    This ending represents pure panic and survival instinct. Choose to run away near the beginning, beg to be let go, select heart rate or physical pain, ask if he plans to let you go, and choose the scared response.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Tackle Warning">
Choosing to run away near the beginning will cause Calvin to tackle you. While this leads to high-tension scenes, it is also a mandatory step to unlock a hidden route.
</Callout>

The hidden route in *Just Be Patient* requires a specific sequence of actions and a manual text input that is not listed in the standard choice menus. 

### The Sore Body and "makemebetter" Sequence

| Step | Action Required | In-Game Outcome |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **1. Trigger Chase** | Run away from Calvin at the beginning of the game. | Calvin tackles you, causing physical soreness. |
| **2. Unlock ??? Option** | Advance to the concerns section later in the story. | A hidden concern dialogue option regarding your sore body becomes selectable. |
| **3. Type Secret Phrase** | Wait until Calvin appears wearing only a black shirt. | A manual text input box will appear on the screen. |
| **4. Input Code** | Type **makemebetter** exactly as shown and submit. | The secret route is unlocked, leading to the hidden ending. |

<Callout type="success" title="Secret Input Verified">
The manual entry **makemebetter** must be typed without quotes, spaces, or capital letters. Ensure your keyboard layout is set correctly before submitting.
