The heart of the **just be patient tutorial** lies in the branching choice system. Every decision you make feeds into a hidden "Attitude Meter" that Calvin tracks. There are four primary ways to interact with him: Cooperation, Questioning, Resistance, and Passivity.

Early choices set the tone, but mid-game decisions regarding your "Main Concern" act as the final gatekeepers for specific endings. For example, focusing on your mental health often leads to "softer" endings, whereas focusing on the restraints or the medication leads to "curious" or "hostile" routes.
