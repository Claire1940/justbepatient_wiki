<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Set the Opening Attitude">
    Your initial reaction to waking up determines the early narrative tone. Choose to politely greet Calvin, confront him angrily, ignore him entirely, or attempt to run.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Define Your Main Concern">
    When asked about your condition, select your focus. Choose mental health to pursue softer routes, the medication for analytical paths, or the physical situation to build resistance.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Take Physical Action">
    Interact with the environment. Decide whether to test your restraints, investigate the computer terminal, refuse food, or ask Calvin directly about his long-term plans.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Deliver the Final Emotional Verdict">
