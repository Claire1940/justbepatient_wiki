<Section icon={Map} title="Just Be Patient Endings: All Routes Overview">

*Just Be Patient* is a dark psychological horror visual novel developed by HexedHoney. The narrative follows a protagonist trapped in an unfamiliar, clinical environment under the absolute control of Calvin, a deeply unsettling doctor. The game features multiple narrative paths shaped by your dialogue choices, physical resistance, and emotional compliance. 

To complete every route, you must experiment with different attitudes toward Calvin. A polite demeanor leads to emotional attachment or compliance, while hostility and escape attempts unlock high-tension horror endings. Below is a high-level overview of the requirements for each ending.

### Endings Reference Table

| Ending Name | Route Style | Primary Choice Path | Key Outcome |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **A Loving Heart** | Polite & Attached | Greet politely, select mental health, express love | Emotional codependency |
| **Compliance** | Polite & Withheld | Greet politely, select mental health, deny love | Psychological submission |
| **A Curious Mind** | Investigative | Confront, check computer, ask about his motives | Intellectual engagement |
| **Resistance** | Hostile & Defiant | Confront, insult, test restraints, choose anger | High-tension confrontation |
| **Mercy** | Fearful & Pleading | Beg for release, refuse food, choose scared | Submissive plea |
| **Passivity** | Quiet & Avoidant | Ignore, ask to stay upstairs, choose tired | Complete withdrawal |
| **Animal** | Panic & Survival | Run at start, plead, focus on pain, choose scared | Visceral fight-or-flight |
| **Secret Ending** | Special Input | Run at start, unlock "sore body", type secret phrase | Hidden truth unlock |

<Callout type="tip" title="Recommended Play Order">
Start with **A Loving Heart** or **Compliance** to understand Calvin's base personality, then proceed to **A Curious Mind** and **Resistance** to test the boundaries of his control. Save the **Animal** and **Secret Ending** routes for last.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Walkthrough: Standard Endings">

To unlock all standard **just be patient visual novel endings**, you must align your choices with a consistent attitude from the very first scene. Use the steps below to systematically reach each narrative conclusion.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="A Loving Heart & Compliance Routes">
    These paths require a soft, cooperative approach. Greet Calvin politely and ask why he is staring. When discussing concerns, focus heavily on your **mental health**. Ask why he chose you, and lean into being tired. 
    - For **A Loving Heart**: Choose to tell him you love him at the final emotional prompt.
    - For **Compliance**: Choose to tell him you do not love him at the final emotional prompt.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="A Curious Mind Route">
    Take an investigative, questioning approach. Confront Calvin immediately and ask why he brought you there. Focus your concerns on the **new medication**. Test your restraints, inspect the computer terminal, and ask how long he has harbored these feelings for you.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Resistance Route">
    Push back violently against his authority. Confront him, use insults, and focus entirely on your current **situation**. Test the restraints aggressively, demand to know if he plans to release you, and select the angry dialogue options whenever they appear.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Mercy & Passivity Routes">
    - **Mercy**: Beg Calvin to let you go, focus on your mental health, refuse to eat the food he brings, and choose the most scared reactions.
    - **Passivity**: Ignore his presence, ask why he brought you there, declare you have no concerns, ask if you can stay upstairs with him, and choose the tired option.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Animal Route">
    This path is driven by pure panic. Choose to run at the very beginning of the game. Once caught, beg to be let go, focus on your heart rate or physical pain, ask if he plans to release you, and select the terrified, scared responses.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Save Slot Warning">
Always create a hard manual save at the "Main Concern" choice menu. This allows you to easily jump between Mental Health, New Medication, and Situation routes without replaying the opening sequence.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="How to Unlock the Secret Ending">

The secret route in *Just Be Patient* deviates from the standard choice menus and requires a specific sequence of physical resistance and a typed-input phrase. 

### Secret Ending Unlock Steps

| Step | Action Required | Narrative Impact |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **1** | Run at the beginning | Calvin tackles the protagonist, causing physical soreness. |
| **2** | Select "Sore Body" concern | This hidden option replaces the locked "???" dialogue choice. |
| **3** | Progress to the black-shirt scene | Play normally until Calvin appears wearing a black shirt. |
| **4** | Type the secret code | Type **makemebetter** when the game prompts you for text input. |
