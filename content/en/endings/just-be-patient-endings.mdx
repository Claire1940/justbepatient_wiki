<Section icon={Swords} title="Step-by-Step Walkthrough: Branching Choice Paths">

Your progression through Calvin's facility is guided by key choice clusters. To reach specific **just be patient endings**, you must understand how early-game reactions lock or unlock later narrative branches. Use this step-by-step path structure to align your choices with your target route.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Select Your Opening Attitude">
    When you first encounter Calvin in the dark, your initial reaction sets the baseline for his behavior.
    - **Polite**: Choose *Politely greet them* to work toward *A Loving Heart* or *Compliance*.
    - **Confrontational**: Choose *Confront them* to pave the way for *A Curious Mind* or *Resistance*.
    - **Avoidant**: Choose *Ignore them* to head down the *Passivity* route.
    - **Survival**: Choose *Run* to trigger the physical struggle required for *Animal* and the secret scenes.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Define Your Main Concern">
    During the mid-game evaluation, Calvin will ask about your primary concerns. This choice acts as a major route filter.
    - **Mental Health**: Directs the narrative toward softer, emotional endings.
    - **New Medication**: Focuses the story on investigation and curiosity.
    - **The Situation**: Increases hostility and pushes toward the escape-focused paths.
    - **No Concerns**: Keeps the protagonist passive and compliant.
    - **Sore Body (???)**: Only appears if you ran at the start, leading to unique scenes.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Take Investigative Action">
    Your physical actions inside the room determine how much leverage you have over Calvin.
    - **Check the Computer**: Unlocks deeper lore and supports the *Curious Mind* ending.
    - **Test the Restraints**: Essential for hostile and resistant routes.
    - **Refuse to Eat**: Signals deep fear and vulnerability, keeping you on the *Mercy* or *Animal* tracks.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Deliver the Final Emotional Response">
    The final dialogue choice is the ultimate decider for the standard endings.
    - **Love**: Triggers *A Loving Heart*.
    - **Rejection**: Triggers *Compliance*.
    - **Anger**: Safely lands you on the *Resistance* ending.
    - **Fear**: Results in *Mercy* or *Animal* depending on early-game physical choices.
    - **Tiredness**: Leads to the *Passivity* conclusion.
  </Step>
</Steps>
