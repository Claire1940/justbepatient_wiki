Every decision in **Just Be Patient doctor** contributes to an internal "Attitude" score. These choices are divided into three main phases: the Opening, the Mid-Route Concerns, and the Final Emotional Choice.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="The Opening Attitude">
    Your first interaction with Calvin sets the baseline. Choosing to **Politely Greet** him opens the path to "A Loving Heart," while **Confronting** him is necessary for "Resistance" or "Curiosity."
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="The Concern Branch">
    When Calvin asks about your concerns, your answer is the primary route filter. **Mental Health** leads to emotional endings; **The New Medication** leads to investigative endings; **The Situation** leads to hostile endings.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Action and Investigation">
    Deciding whether to **Test the Restraints** or **Check the Computer** provides additional context. Checking the computer is mandatory for the "Curious Mind" ending.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="The Final Emotional Choice">
    The final prompt—choosing between Love, Anger, Fear, or Tiredness—is the ultimate decider for the ending splash screen.

</Steps>
